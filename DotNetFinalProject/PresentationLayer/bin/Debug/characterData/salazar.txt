-------------------------------------------------
Extra Attack: Whenever you take the attack action
on your turn, you can make two attacks instead of
one, as a part of taking that action.

Second Wind: You have a limited well of stamina 
that you can draw on to protect yourself from 
harm. On your turn, you can use a bonus action to 
regain hit points equal to 1d10 + your fighter 
level. Once you use this feature, you must finish 
a short or long rest before you can use it again.

Fighting Style (Archery): You gain a +2 bonus to 
attack rolls you make with ranged weapons.

Rustic Hospitality: Since you come from the ranks 
of the common folk, you fit in among them with 
ease. You can find a place to hide, rest, or 
recuperate among other commoners, unless you have 
shown yourself to be a danger to them. They will 
shield you from the law or anyone else searching 
for you, though they will not risk their lives 
for you.

Shield: When shield is equiped AC jumps from 17
to 19, shield can only be equiped while using a
one handed weapon
